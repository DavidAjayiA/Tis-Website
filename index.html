Emerging Financial Leaders

The Investment Society is the biggest student society in Sub-Saharan Africa and focuses on simulating real-world finance. It aims to bridge the gap between theory and practical aspect of finance.

Our Programs

Unlock your investment potential through targeted initiatives

Insight Days

Gain firsthand knowledge from seasoned professionals representing diverse corners of the investment world to put theory into practice and build valuable connections with like-minded individuals.

The Colloquium

Join fellow members for an open forum on current trends and topics that piques your curiosity. Share your strategies, and let the conversation flow.

General Body Meetings

Showcase your analytical prowess and research skills through participation in prestigious challenges like the CFA Institute Research Challenge and so on.
