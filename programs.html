Our Programs and Meetings

Stay Connected — Explore Our Programs, Activities, and Gatherings

Programs for Our Members

1

Daily Research Meetings & Market Updates

Members meet daily to analyze market activities, share insights, and discuss the latest financial trends across local and global markets.

2

Weekly Divisional Meetings

Focused sessions where divisions collaborate on research projects, share performance analyses, and enhance their analytical skills.

3

Weekly General Body Meetings

Open sessions where all members engage in discussions on market updates, strategy presentations, and society-wide announcements.

4

Weekly Interview Preparation Sessions

Workshops to help members develop professional resumes, improve interview skills, and prepare for internships or graduate roles.

5

Monthly Industry Speaker Sessions

Industry professionals and CEOs share insights on finance, investment, and corporate life, connecting theory with real-world practice.

★

Special Annual Events

Includes Industry Office Visits, Financial Literacy Week, and the Annual Colloquium Conference to foster growth and networking.

At Our Meetings

  • We analyze and review market movements across equities, currencies, commodities, and fixed-income instruments.
  • We discuss macroeconomic trends, policies, and their global impact.
  • We analyze financial reports of local and international companies.
  • We simulate investment bank M&A and capital-raising processes for practical understanding.
  • We explore investment strategies such as stock trading and venture investing.
  • We develop presentation, confidence, and critical thinking skills through active participation.
